# Contract Policies

This document outlines the policies for contracts in the Pact Contract Catalog.

## Licensing
- All contracts must be licensed under Apache-2.0 or a compatible open-source license.
- License must be clearly stated in metadata.

## Quality Standards
- Contracts must follow Pact best practices.
- Include comprehensive documentation.
- Provide test coverage for all functions.

## Security
- Contracts must not contain known vulnerabilities.
- Security WG review required for complex contracts.
- External audits may be mandated.

## Maintenance
- Contracts must be actively maintained.
- Updates for breaking changes in dependencies.
- Deprecation notices for outdated contracts.

## Compliance
- Adhere to PCO Code of Conduct.
- No discriminatory or harmful content.
- Respect intellectual property rights.

## Review Process
- All submissions undergo automated and manual review.
- Core maintainers have final approval.
- Appeals can be made to the Council.

Violations may result in contract removal from the catalog.
